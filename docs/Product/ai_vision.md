# AI Vision

## Purpose

This document captures every AI idea for the Luxe Haven Collective platform.

Ideas do **not** need to be fully designed before being added.

The goal is to build an AI platform that helps operators make better decisions, automate repetitive work, and deliver exceptional guest experiences.

---

# Guiding Principles

AI should...

- Save time.
- Increase revenue.
- Improve guest satisfaction.
- Reduce operational overhead.
- Assist decision making.
- Never replace human judgment for critical business decisions.

Every AI feature should answer one question:

> "How does this make operating a hospitality business easier?"

---

# AI Platform Architecture

Future AI capabilities will be organized into specialized agents.

Rather than one chatbot, Luxe Haven will include multiple purpose-built AI assistants.

Example:

Revenue AI

↓

Listing AI

↓

Guest AI

↓

Operations AI

↓

Owner AI

↓

Business AI

Each agent has a clearly defined responsibility.
